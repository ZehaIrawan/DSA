Don’t solve the **same exact LeetCode problem every day**. That’s mostly *memorization*, not skill.

Better approach:

* Solve it **once**
* Understand the pattern (two pointers, hashmap, binary search, etc.)
* Then solve **3–5 similar problems**
* Come back to the original problem **after a few days** and redo it from scratch

This builds real problem-solving ability because you’re training your brain to recognize patterns, not recall answers.

**Best method: spaced repetition**

* Day 1: solve + fully understand
* Day 2: solve a similar one
* Day 4: solve another similar one
* Day 7: redo the original problem without looking

That’s the fastest way to improve.
